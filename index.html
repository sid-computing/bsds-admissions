01 / Admissions

One process,
made understandable.

This website is intended primarily for Class XI and XII students interested in pursuing the Bachelor of Statistical Data Science at the Indian Statistical Institute.

The admission guide will organise official notices into one readable sequence—from checking eligibility and applying, to interpreting merit lists, submitting centre preferences and responding to seat allocations.

Admission mode

Maximum Mathematics percentile from JEE Main or CUET-UG

The Mathematics percentile used for admission is the higher of the candidate’s eligible JEE Main and CUET-UG Mathematics percentiles, subject to the rules published for the relevant admission cycle.

Annual intake

Three centres.
Twenty-five seats each.

The programme is offered at the ISI Calcutta, ISI Delhi and ISI Bengaluru centres.

Each centre has an annual intake of 25 students, giving a total annual intake of 75 students across the programme.

05 / Before you join

Arrive curious,
not overwhelmed.

You do not need to finish an entire university curriculum before joining. A strong foundation and a willingness to learn are more useful than rushing through advanced material.

Mathematics

Revise algebra, functions, coordinate geometry, calculus and basic combinatorial reasoning.

Programming

Basic familiarity with variables, conditionals, loops and functions in Python or R can make the transition easier.

Statistics

Develop intuition for data, averages, variation, probability and interpreting graphs.

Study habits

Be prepared for proof-based mathematics, programming practice, projects and learning concepts independently.

How to use this resource

Official facts.
Student context.
Clear separation.

Official notices and published data will be cited and dated. Commentary, interpretation and personal advice will be clearly identified so that readers can distinguish evidence from opinion.
