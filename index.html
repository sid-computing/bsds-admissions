01 / Admissions

One process,
made understandable.

This website is for Class XI and XII students who wish to pursue the Bachelor of Statistical Data Science at the Indian Statistical Institute.

The admission guide will bring official notices into one readable sequence—from checking eligibility and applying, to interpreting merit lists and responding to seat allocations.

How to use this resource

Official facts.
Student context.
Clear separation.

Official notices and published data will be cited and dated. Commentary, interpretation and personal advice will be clearly identified, so students can tell evidence from opinion.

Past ranks and allocations can provide context, but they cannot guarantee what will happen in a future admission cycle.
